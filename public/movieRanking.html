Best Movies According to Stuart

Top 5 Movies

Ghost in the Shell

Japanese cyberpunk anime that put the loonie tunes of the past in the bin.

The Good the Bad and the Ugly

Gritty Western set during the American Civil War.

The Matrix

Original sci-fi flick with martial arts and cool filming technique.

Infernal Affairs

Edge-of-the-seat Hong Kong police drama.

Blade Runner

A unique, noir sci-fi with steam punk visuals.
